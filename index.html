Our Services

🏠 Airbnb Cleaning

Our Airbnb cleaning service is tailored for short-let properties requiring fast turnaround and high presentation standards. We ensure your property is guest-ready after every stay.

  • Full surface cleaning and sanitisation
  • Bed making and linen change (if provided)
  • Bathroom deep clean and disinfection
  • Kitchen cleaning including appliances and worktops
  • Restocking essentials (on request)
  • Final inspection to ensure hotel-level presentation

Deep cleaning is also available.

📦 End of Tenancy Cleaning

Our end of tenancy cleaning is a comprehensive deep clean designed to meet landlord and letting agent standards, helping tenants secure their deposit return.

  • Deep cleaning of all rooms including skirting boards and corners
  • Kitchen degreasing (oven, hob, extractor, cupboards)
  • Full bathroom descaling and sanitisation
  • Internal window cleaning
  • Carpet vacuuming and floor mopping
  • Removal of limescale, stains, and built-up dirt
  • Detailed checklist-based cleaning to ensure nothing is missed

Deep cleaning is also available.

🏢 Commercial Cleaning

We provide reliable commercial cleaning services tailored to offices, shops, and business environments, ensuring a clean and professional workspace.

  • Workstation and desk cleaning
  • Floor care (vacuuming, mopping, polishing)
  • Washroom cleaning and restocking
  • Kitchen and communal area cleaning
  • Dusting and sanitising high-touch areas
  • Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, or custom)

Deep cleaning is also available.

🧼 Domestic Cleaning

Our domestic cleaning service keeps your home consistently clean, comfortable, and hygienic with attention to detail in every visit.

  • General dusting and surface cleaning
  • Kitchen cleaning including worktops and appliances
  • Bathroom cleaning and sanitisation
  • Vacuuming and mopping floors
  • Cleaning under furniture where accessible
  • Customised cleaning based on your preferences

Deep cleaning is also available.
